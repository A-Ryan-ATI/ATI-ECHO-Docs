# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 10/04/2026
## 1. Introduction  
Welcome to ATI-ECHO (“we”, “our”, or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to operate primarily **offline**, and we do not collect or transmit your personal data to external servers.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect  
We do **not** collect, store, or share any personal information, including but not limited to:
- Names, email addresses, or contact details  
- Location data  
- Audio recordings or transcriptions  
- Device identifiers or analytics data  

All data generated within the app remains on your device.

---

## 3. On-Device Data Processing  
ATI-ECHO processes the following data **entirely on your device**:
- Audio recordings captured via your device’s microphone  
- Transcriptions generated from recorded audio  
- Summaries created from transcriptions  

This data is:
- Stored locally on your device  
- Not transmitted to any external servers  
- Not accessible by us or any third parties  

---

## 4. Permissions We Use  

### Microphone Access  
Used to record audio for transcription and summarisation. Audio is processed locally and never leaves your device.

### Storage Access  
Used to save audio recordings, transcriptions, and summary reports on your device.

---

## 5. Internet Access  
The app may access the internet **only during initial setup** to download machine learning models required for on-device functionality.

- No personal data or user-generated content is transmitted during this process  
- After setup, the app can function entirely offline  

---

## 6. Data Sharing  
We do **not**:
- Share data with third parties  
- Sell user data  
- Use analytics or tracking tools  

---

## 7. Data Retention  
All data (audio, transcriptions, summaries) is stored locally on your device and remains under your control. You can delete this data at any time by removing it from your device or uninstalling the app.

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy  
This app is not specifically directed at children under 13. However, since we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of data collection from children.

---

## 9. Security  
Because all data is stored locally on your device and not transmitted, the security of your data depends on your device’s security measures.

---

## 10. Your Rights  
Since we do not collect or store your data, we do not have access to it and cannot modify or delete it on your behalf. You retain full control of your data on your device.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy  
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on the app’s listing page.
